Background Story

Meet Sahsa! She's a very cute Husky. She was the runt of her litter and abandoned by her family. You came across her while going on your daily run, when you saw her under a bridge. It took a while for her to trust you enough to take her home, but sure enough youve formed a bond. She can be a bit emotional at times, but you love her all the same!
